How it works
Three steps. No friction.
01
Add your style guide
Paste your writing rules as plain text, or upload a .txt or .md file. Load an example — journalism, business, newsletter, or Chuck Hemann's Style Guide — to see it in action, or paste your own.
02
Paste your draft
Drop in any piece of writing — blog post, LinkedIn update, newsletter, chapter, anything. Hit Check Style. Claude reads both documents and identifies every rule your draft breaks.
03
Fix with one click
Violations are ranked by severity. Each one shows exactly what's wrong, why it matters, and a specific proposed rewrite. Clear violations apply automatically. Complex ones show you where to look.
